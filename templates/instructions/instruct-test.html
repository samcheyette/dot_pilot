In this experiment, you are going to see a novel object called a "blicket" and be asked to categorize other objects as blickets or not. Some objects are blickets and others are not, and the features you can see (color, shape, size, stripeness, girth) determine what a blicket is. It's your job to figure out what features make a blicket a blicket. We ask that you give your full attention to the task while participating.
